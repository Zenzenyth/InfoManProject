Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility requirements for a BPI Credit Card?

Applicants must be at least 21 years old, a Filipino citizen or a foreign resident with a valid visa, and meet minimum income requirements. Other criteria may apply.

How many supplementary cards can I apply for with my credit card?

You can apply for a maximum of 2 supplementary cards per credit card.

How long does the application process take?

Processing time usually takes 5-7 banking days, provided all documents are complete. You will be notified of your application status.

Can I apply for a supplementary card?

Yes, you can. The supplementary cardholder must be at least 13 years old, and you can apply for up to 2 supplementary cards per credit card.
